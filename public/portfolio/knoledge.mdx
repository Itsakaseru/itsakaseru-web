Knoledge is a web application designed for educational institutions to manage students and staff according to their academic system. Teachers can assign scores, manage re-review requests, and monitor student performance throughout their education, while students can log in to view their scores and progress. This project was developed as part of our Web Programming final project.

I led the team for this project, overseeing both the design and development process. I worked primarily on the UI/UX sketch design for the web application, which was later refined, beautify and finalized by my teammate, and also contributed to backend development alongside the team, ensuring smooth integration between frontend and backend components.

This project is one of my proudest achievements, as it involved a larger and more complex scope compared to my previous projects. Developing a full-stack web application using CodeIgniter 3 challenged me to handle both design and development for a real-world educational platform, giving me valuable hands-on experience in building scalable web applications.
