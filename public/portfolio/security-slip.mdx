Security Slip — or as we like to call it, SSL (lol) — is a simulation game where your job is to make sure everyone who comes in… also comes ou— I mean, um… has the proper entry cards. Inspired by Papers, Please, we asked ourselves: since it’s a 2D game, why not turn it into a 3D experience with a bit more twist?

This game was developed as part of our Game Design and Development 2 final project. Due to the limited development timeframe, several planned game components are still not yet implemented, leaving room for future expansion and refinement.

In this project, I led the team, guiding both design and development efforts. I worked primarily on the game design, including UI, UX, and the Game Design Document (GDD) in collaboration with a teammate, while also contributing to the backend development alongside the rest of the team, helping bring the core gameplay systems to life.
